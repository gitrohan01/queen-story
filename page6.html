Chapter 6 — The Queen’s Victory

The battlefield thundered with the sound of triumph. The queen stood tall, her banner raised high — not as a symbol of power, but of hope, strength, and every sacrifice she’d endured.

She had won. Not because fate favored her… but because she fought for her people with a heart unshakable.

From a distant cliff, the soldier watched with quiet pride.
“She rose,” he whispered. “My Queen truly rose.”
