Final Chapter — The Crown and The Heart

Dawn broke gently over the kingdom, the first light warming the queen’s face as she stood upon her balcony. She had risen — not just to power, but to purpose.

Beyond the castle walls, beneath the same sunrise, the soldier watched with quiet pride. Their paths were no longer beside each other, yet their hearts remained connected in ways the world could never understand.

She whispered into the morning breeze:
“Wherever you are… thank you for believing in me.”

And from afar, he answered softly:
“Always, my Queen.”
