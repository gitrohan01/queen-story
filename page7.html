Chapter 7 — The Soldier’s Silent Pride

Fireworks burst above the castle, painting the sky in colors of celebration. The kingdom rejoiced — their queen had triumphed.

Yet far from the noise, the soldier stood on a quiet cliff, watching with a soft smile. Not of envy, but of fulfillment.

“You were meant for greatness,” he whispered to the wind.

She ruled the world with strength and grace.
And he… admired her from afar, proud beyond words.
