Chapter 4 — The Princess’s Secret

Beneath falling cherry blossoms, the princess found the soldier alone in the royal garden. The air shimmered with quiet magic as she stepped closer.

Her voice trembled:

“There is something I haven’t told you…”

She took a breath, heart racing:

“Your vow… it moved something inside me. Something I didn’t know existed.”

And for the first time, the soldier realized — she cared for him too.
