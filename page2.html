Chapter 2 — The Hidden Letter

Later that night, under the soft glow of candlelight, the princess unrolled the scroll once more. Between its folds, she discovered a second message — hidden… intentional… meant only for her eyes.

The handwriting was nervous yet sincere:

“I protect you not out of duty… but out of devotion.”

Her heart felt something unfamiliar — warmth, curiosity, and a flutter she never expected from a soldier’s vows.
