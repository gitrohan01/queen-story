Rise, My Queen

The young warrior stood before his queen — not bowing this time, but standing strong. His armor carried the dust of battles, yet his eyes held nothing but belief.

"My Queen," he said softly, "you were born to lead kingdoms, not follow shadows."

"There will be days when doubt tries to conquer you… when the world feels heavy. But remember this — even the fiercest storms kneel before mountains that refuse to move."

"You are that mountain. You are the crown that destinies bow to."

He placed his hand over his heart.
"Do your best. Lead with strength. Walk with fire. Rule the world with a heart no fear can break."

"And know this — no matter where my duty takes me… my heart will always find its way back to you. You are not just my Queen — you are the reason my sword rises, the reason my soul stays warm."
