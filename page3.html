Chapter 3 — The Moonlit Promise

Under the silent glow of moonlight, their paths crossed again on the castle terrace. The princess stepped forward, unsure yet drawn to him by a force she couldn’t name.

“Why do you protect me so fiercely?” she whispered.

His answer floated gently on the night air:
“Because your smile feels like home.”

And under the moonlit sky, a quiet promise aligned their destinies.
