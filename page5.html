Chapter 5 — The Queen Who Rose

The princess grew into a queen not by chance, but by strength. Challenges sharpened her. Choices shaped her. Courage crowned her long before the kingdom did.

And though the throne was heavy, she sat upon it with a grace the world admired.

In the quiet shadows behind her, the soldier watched — calm, steady, proud.

She did not need him to lead.
She only needed him to believe.
